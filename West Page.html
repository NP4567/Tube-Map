Strawberry Hill House

Strawberry Hill House is the Gothic Revival villa what was built in Twickenham, London by Horace Walpole (1717-1797) from 1749 onward. It is the type example of the "Strawberry Hill Gothic" style or architecture, and it prefigured the nineteenth-century Gothic revival.

Osterley Park House

Osterley Park is a mansion set in a large park of the same name. It is in the London Borough of Hounslow, part of the western suburbs of London. It served as a country retreat for wealthy families. In the 2012 Batman film "The Dark Knight Rises", the interior of Osterley Park mansion is used as a double for Wayne Manor.
