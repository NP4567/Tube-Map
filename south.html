Cutty Sark

Cutty Sark is known as the world's last surviving tea clipper, which was one of the fastest and greatest during its time. It is perched on its glass pedestal on Greenwichs water's edge. You can also discover its famous history and cargoes which have been filled with tea, wisky and other commodities at that time. It also offers the largest worldwide collection of merchant-ship figureheads.
